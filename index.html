Short Bio and Work History
Jonathan Pan is currently Founder of Jogo Labs, a metaverse venture studio. Prevoiusly, he was Head of Strategy and Operations at Meta Gaming. Jon has also spent four years at Amazon in various roles (including Head of Esports, Head of Content) across multiple teams (Amazon Games, Prime Gaming, Amazon Kids).

His first post-MBA job was Product Manager at Riot Games. He also co-founded and was the CEO of Ember, an esports team. Prior to Stern, Jonathan was a Senior Consultant at Ernst & Young. He is a veteran of the War in Afghanistan where he served as a infantry officer in the U.S. Army.
